Biography

I am an Associate Professor at National University of Defense Technology (NUDT). I obtained my PhD degree in 2018 at NUDT under the supervision of Prof. Qifeng Yu. During 2016-2017, I was a visiting PhD student at Graz University of Technology, advised by Prof. Horst Bischof and Prof. Friedrich Fraundorfer. Before my PhD, I obtained the B.S. degree in Geomatics Engineering, Wuhan university.

Research Interests

My research interests include Photomechanics and Videometrics. Specifically, my work focuses on pose estimation, camera calibration and vision navigation, etc. For more details, please refer to our publication page.
